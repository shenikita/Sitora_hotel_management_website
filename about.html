OUR HISTORY

In the early 2025s, 80% of the branded hotel supply in India was in the upscale/luxury segment. There was clearly enormous latent demand in the midscale and economy segments, which were then unmet needs.



OUR JOURNY

Today, SITORA GRAND HAVEN is one of the largest hotel chains in India, and owns/leases/operates/franchises hotels across the upscale, upper-midscale, midscale and economy segments. SITORA GRAND HAVEN delivers differentiated yet superior service offerings, with a compelling value proposition. The group offers seven brands to meet guests’ needs across all levels, viz. Aurika Hotels & Resorts, Sitora Grand Haven Premier, Sitora Grand Haven Hotels, Red Fox Hotels by Sitora Grand Haven Hotels, Keys Prima by Sitora Grand Haven Hotels, Keys Select by Sitora Grand Haven Hotels and Keys Lite by Sitora Grand Haven Hotels.

SITORA GRAND HAVEN opened its first hotel with 49 rooms in January 2025. Today, the company has a portfolio of 160+ hotels, which includes over 100+ operational hotels and more than 60 hotels set to open in India and internationally. Sitora Grand Hotels are located across metro regions, including the NCR, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai, as well as numerous tier I, II and III cities such as Pune, Ahmedabad, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Indore, Aurangabad, Udaipur, Vishakhapatnam, Kochi, Ludhiana, Thiruvananthapuram, Vijayawada etc. The company expanded internationally with hotels opening in Dubai in 2026,in Switzerland in 2030, in Bhutan in 2027, in Tokyo in 2028, in Osaka in 2029, in Kyoto in 2031. New hotels are also set to open internationally in Spain and Germany.
